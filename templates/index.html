COVID-19 Impact on BMI

Physical Activity Changes

Lockdowns and restrictions led to reduced physical activity and increased sedentary behavior, contributing to weight gain in many individuals.

Dietary Changes

Stress eating, limited access to fresh foods, and changes in eating patterns during lockdowns affected weight management.

Direct Disease Impact

COVID-19 infection itself can affect metabolism, appetite, and muscle mass, leading to significant weight changes in some patients.

Mental Health Effects

Anxiety, depression, and stress during the pandemic influenced eating habits and weight management behaviors.
