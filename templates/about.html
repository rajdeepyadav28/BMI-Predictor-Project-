Our Mission

We are committed to leveraging the power of artificial intelligence and machine learning to provide accurate, accessible, and actionable health insights. Our goal is to help individuals and healthcare professionals better understand the long-term impacts of COVID-19 on body weight and overall health, enabling more informed decisions and better health outcomes for everyone.
